About me

Hi again! My name is Sharlene Fang and I'm currently third year student at the University of Washington Seattle. I am majoring in Informatics with a focus in Human Computer Interaction as I strive to become a UX designer in the future. I love that through the UI/UX field, I can take my passion of design and combine it with psychology and technology to bring my art to life and innovate solutions to improve the lives of others. I aim to not only generate positive user experience, but also become a responsible and reflective designer that is capable of creating diverse and inclusive products.
